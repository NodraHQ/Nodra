# Nodra

> Knowledge before capital.

Nodra is an open education platform focused on blockchain, DeFi and digital finance.

Our goal is simple:

- Teach everything for free.
- Help people apply that knowledge in the real world.
- Build an open community around digital finance.

---

## Products

### 📚 Nodra Academy

A free learning platform designed to help anyone understand blockchain from the ground up.

Current learning paths:

- Blockchain
- Wallets
- Networks
- Digital Assets
- DeFi
- Lending
- Yield
- Security
- On-chain
- Real World

## Philosophy

We believe education should be accessible.

Knowledge comes before capital.

Instead of selling expensive courses, Nodra focuses on providing free, high-quality educational content while offering personalized guidance for people who want help applying that knowledge.
